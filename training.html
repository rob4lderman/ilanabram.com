Animal Training

I am fascinated with animal learning and all the ways in which animal behavior can be influenced. I love seeing animals thrive, and I love sharing my training tools with other humans.

I am currently enrolled in the Karen Pryor Academy for Animal Training and Behavior. I will be graduating from their 6-month Dog Training Professional program in August, 2013.

The program is designed around Clicker Training, a science-based training method that uses positive reinforcement in conjunction with a clicker. Clicker training gives me the ability to fulfill a childhood dream: talking to animals in a language they can understand.

I've studied Dr. Sophia Yin's materials on counterconditioning and management, complementing my "operant-conditioning" focus from the Karen Pryor Academy. I'm also influenced by Temple Grandin, who gave me important insight into the emotional needs of animals.

I love training dogs, but my passion is animal training in general. I'm currently interning at the Trevor Zoo, shadowing staff and learning the ropes while creating and implementing training plans for various animals. So far, I've had a few targeting sessions with Betty the Bobcat. I am very grateful for this amazing opportunity!
